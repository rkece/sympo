About RMKEC

R.M.K. Engineering College, established in the year 1995, is an autonomous institution receiving accolades for its quality education. Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

Accredited by NAAC with A+ Grade, we strive to foster innovation and technical excellence. The Department of ECE takes pride in organizing HONEKEN 2026, a platform for heavy-duty technical combat.
